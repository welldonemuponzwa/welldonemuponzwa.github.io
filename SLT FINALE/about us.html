Welcome to SACMAR LEAF TOBACCO

About Us

SACMAR LEAF TOBACCO (Pvt) Ltd is a Zimbabwean company which was registered in 2023. It is licensed with The Tobacco Industry and Marketing Board (TIMB) as a Class “A” buyer, and the license permit the company to buy tobacco in Zimbabwe for export. Since 2023 the company has been buying tobacco on the auction floors on behalf of other tobacco merchants. The company is involved in tobacco contracting, processing and exporting.

Our Mission

We are dedicated to fostering strong partnerships with farmers, suppliers, and clients by ensuring the responsible sourcing, processing, and distribution of premium tobacco. Our commitment to quality, sustainability, and ethical practices drives us to deliver excellence while respecting the environment and empowering the communities we work with.

Our Vision

To be the leading provider of responsibly sourced and sustainably produced tobacco, setting industry benchmarks for quality, innovation, and ethical practices while building lasting value for our stakeholders and the communities we serve
